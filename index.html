His Divine Grace

A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada

"Real happiness comes from within. It is not to be found in the external, material world."

In 1965, at age 69, Srila Prabhupada traveled from India to America with just $8 and a trunk of sacred books. Within 12 years, he established 108 temples worldwide and translated over 70 volumes of Vedic literature, making Krishna consciousness accessible to millions.
